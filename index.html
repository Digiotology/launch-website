Digiotology is the innovation and transformation network where ideas come alive.

We have an inventive approach to helping businesses achieve their innovation and transformation aspirations. Because every business is different, we follow a customised design methodology that is applicable whether you are at the earliest stage of idea development or further along on your journey. We practice what we preach, so you’ll find us nimble in how we work with you, always mindful of your need to demonstrate ROI fast; combining solid, trusted industry expertise with a creative and inventive twist.

We are highly connected with a well-established, extensive network of disruptive thinkers, developers, data scientists, academics, technologists and innovators. Digiotology has grown organically from Nimbus Ninety, the independent community of disruptive business and technology leaders, forming a natural bridge for digital leaders ready to move beyond research and understanding, to taking proactive steps to fast track innovation and transformation.

Digiotology adheres to the same rigorous research principles and endeavours to become the market voice for the new digital vanguard of programmers, data scientists, designers and innovators. Our team’s extensive knowledge and experience has led to the development of the Digiotology innovation methodology; a unique customised design methodology, developed in partnership with our network.

  • Explore

    Discover a future of infinite possibilities

    The first stage in the journey is understanding what you need and how we can work together to help you achieve your innovation and transformation aspirations. In most cases we’ll start by spending some time helping you to refine, frame and prioritise what it is you want to do. It’ll be important to get a handle of what data you have and how it could be properly leveraged so we might run a full audit of your data as a starting point. We’ll want to deep dive into your world to make sure we really understand what’s achievable and discover what changes in the organisation might need to occur to enable success. We’ve found the Nimbus Ninety digital benchmarking survey is a really useful tool to help us to do just that.

  • Plan

    Turning your ideas into opportunity

    Once we’ve fully understood your aspirations and assessed your resources and capabilities, we’ll prepare a bespoke plan defining what’s achievable. We’ll also give you some feedback on the organisational changes we think you’ll need to make to really optimise the potential of the project. And should you chose to engage with us on helping you through your transformation project, you’ll find we also have the skills and expertise to help you build the strategy, capabilities, skills and culture. This will be your first glimpse of the possible, and we’ll be able to share some relevant client case studies with you so you can see how similar initiatives have worked in other organisations we’ve helped.

  • Build

    Let’s get to work

    The build is when it all starts getting exciting as this is where your ideas come alive. It’s an iterative process and when we roll up our sleeves and get our hands dirty. This is what we do best; reaching out to our extensive network of data scientists, programmers and designers and developing ideas and plans into prototypes, APIs, workshops and solutions. It’s a pretty inspiring part of the process and everyone starts to get very excited about the possibilities.

  • Experiment

    Dare to be brave and just give it a go

    Now’s the time to be brave and just give it a go. It’s a bold step, but daring to try and encouraging ‘heroic failure’ in your teams is actually seen by experts as the best competitive advantage and it’s how things are done in start-ups where this mentality is hardwired into their DNA. Changing your organisation’s culture can’t be done overnight, but we can guide you through changes that will have a powerful and lasting impact on your ability to bring your ideas to life.

  • Launch

    Prototyping as we go

    This is when we’ll introduce a prototype, like a 3D version of your vision, to the market. It’s not going to be perfect and that’s ok because we’ll be able to test, learn and refine as we go and in a live environment. Depending on your skills sets resources and capabilities, we’ll either enable and complement your teams to do this themselves, or build capacity and do more as you require.

  • Learn

    Regroup, review, re-evaluate, refine

    Now’s the time to regroup to gather, review and evaluate data and feedback from your prototype launch and decide on further refinements to hone product and processes.
